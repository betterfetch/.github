## Why betterfetch?

- **Instant** - boots in milliseconds with zero unnecessary overhead
- **Clean** - presents exactly what you need, nothing more
- **Rust** - modern, fast, and dependency-managed
- **Tiny** - minimal footprint, maximal utility
